Frequently Asked Questions

What are the theory test pass rates by region?
Theory pass rates vary by region: London ~45%, Midlands ~48%, North ~42%, South ~47%. Book during quieter months for better focus.
How long in advance should I book practical tests?
You can book up to 2 months in advance. Popular slots fill quickly. Early morning and mid-week are often available longer.
What if I need to cancel or reschedule?
You can reschedule up to 3 working days before your test with a £11 fee. Cancellations within 3 days forfeit the fee.
How long are test results valid?
Theory test results are valid for 2 years. You must pass practical within this validity period to receive your license.
What documents do I need for the test?
Bring your driving test pass certificate, UK photocard, and proof of address. Arrive 10 minutes early.
Are there any restrictions on booking?
You must hold a valid provisional license and be at least 17 years old. Practical tests require theory test pass first.
